Welcome to Nature Cosmetics

Our mission is straightforward: to craft affordable skincare solutions infused with potent, active superfood ingredients that truly transform your skin.

Our products are meticulously designed with your skin's well-being in mind, ensuring optimal performance while staying true to our commitment to the planet. With Nature Cosmetics, you can experience the joy of clear, radiant skin without compromising your conscience.

Who are we?

Amid the pandemic that affected the entire world, Melbourne had the Izabella Rios was looking for ways to maintain her mental health amid the chaos the world was experiencing. Passionate about cosmetics, Izabella dedicated years working in a large cosmetics industry in Brazil and then decided to deepen her knowledge in cosmetic formulations.

She finished her course in cosmetic formulation and began acquiring ingredients and equipment to test her knowledge. With simple equipment and the desire to create personal care products, the first formulations generated more confidence and the desire to create more and more. Care for the environment meant that the formulations were simple and with vegan ingredients.

The world was returning to normal, it was time to see friends and loved ones and with that came feedback from friends who used them and increasingly asked for the products to go on the market. It was during this period that Izabella saw an opportunity to present to the world a brand that thinks about skin care and the environment in which we live.Thus, Bella Cosmetics was born. A cosmetics brand that is concerned about the environment, with 100% vegan formulas and no animal testing. An independent brand created with great care and quality for a moment of you with you.

In 2023, online sales and local fairs began in Melbourne.
